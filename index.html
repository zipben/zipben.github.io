Wick and Wander

Wick and Wander is an immersive Tabletop Role-Playing Game designed for 2-6 players and one Lore Keeper. Drawing inspiration from various Powered by the Apocalypse games, but featuring a unique card-based resolution system, the game emphasizes exploration and player relationships over combat, creating intense narrative adventures.

The world is one of pastoral abundance where we find humanity, having recovered from the climate apocalypse, learning to live in harmony with an enigmatic force known as the weave.

Human communities huddle around a network of stabilizing artifacts known as the singing stones, which protect them from the chaos of the wild world outside.

Our players are from one of many such communities dotting the now verdant landscape. While the world outside is dangerous, it is also beautiful, vast, and full of wonder.
