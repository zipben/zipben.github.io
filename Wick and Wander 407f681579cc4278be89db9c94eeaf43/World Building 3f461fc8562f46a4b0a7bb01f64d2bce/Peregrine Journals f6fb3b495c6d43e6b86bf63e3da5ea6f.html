Peregrine Journals

A peregrine is a traveler who has chosen to not be tied to any one enclave. They travel the stone roads between communities, bringing news from the farthest edges of The Horn. They have seen more of the world than most, and so some communities give them special advisory positions on their council.

Communities also often maintain a Peregrine Archive, which is a set of regionally specific journals that compile the knowledge of Peregrines who have visited the community. These are akin to travel guides for specific regions in The Horn.

Each journal is divided into three sections, Cautions, Musings and Myths.

Cautions are things that travelers should be wary of, or take steps to avoid

Musings are observations about creatures or points of interest that are unlikely to be dangerous

Myths are stories about singular places or creatures that some might not believe are real

Each journal comes with a thread for the Lore Keeper to draw inspiration from.
