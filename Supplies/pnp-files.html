Print and Play Files

The game uses a unique card driven resolution mechanic. while we are developing the game print at home cards or proxies are the easiest way to give it a try. The cards in this PDF are slightly smaller than average to minimize ink and paper usage. If you have sleeves that can help with the playtesting experience

Alternatively, you may proxy the game using something like Magic the Gathering. MTG is well suited as a proxy since you can use lands as the basic attributes. The breakdown of cards per player is as follows (excluding the lore keeper who does not have a deck)

Lore: ~7 cards per player
Empathy: ~7 cards per player
Harmony: ~7 cards per player
Grace: ~7 cards per player
Power: ~7 cards per player
Injury: ~3 cards per player
Relic: ~1 cards per player
Stich: ~4 cards per player (only required for longer multi season campaigns)
Omens: ~3 cards
