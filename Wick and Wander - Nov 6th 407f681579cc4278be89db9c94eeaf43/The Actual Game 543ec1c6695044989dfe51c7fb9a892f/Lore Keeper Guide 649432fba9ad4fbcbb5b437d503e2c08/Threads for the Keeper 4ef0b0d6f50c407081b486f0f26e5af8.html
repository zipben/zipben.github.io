Threads for the Keeper

A thread is something a lore keeper uses to guide their narrative. They are intended to help fill the world of Wick and Wander with life. Tug on threads often. You don’t need to use the results, but let yourself be inspired by them.

Draw 1-3 cards from your Lore Keepers Deck. Duplicates should compound. Two Lore cards on a travel thread? Something both broken and ancient, or something so ancient it’s become myth.

Travel

Lore - Something ancient or something broken

Empathy - Something gentle or something small

Grace - Something awe inspiring or something fleeting

Power - Something dangerous or something vast

Harmony - Something that changes you or something that grounds you

Seed - Something that reminds them of home. Tie the thread into the narrative.

Food

Lore - Something that tells a story

Empathy - Something that needs to be shared

Grace - Something fresh

Power - Something spicy

Harmony - Something from the earth

Seed - Something someone used to make them all the time

Weather

Lore - Something rare

Empathy - Something in the distance

Grace - Something that appears without warning

Power - Something that sends you running for shelter

Harmony - Something leaves its mark on the landscape

Seed - Something they survived as a child

Person

Lore - Someone stuck in their own head

Empathy - Someone who shares your pain

Grace - Someone in a hurry

Power - Someone intimidating

Harmony - Someone heading your way

Seed - Someone you have in common
